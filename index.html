My Favorite Movie

My Favorite Movie is The Shawshank Redemption.

Storyline: Andy Dufresne, a successful banker, is arrested for the murders of his wife and her lover, and is sentenced to life imprisonment at the Shawshank prison. He becomes the most unconventional prisoner.

Why it is the best movie ever?It has it all - good story, great cast, best footage.

Quote from the movie: "Some birds aren’t meant to be caged. Their feathers are just too bright and when they fly away, the part of you that knows it was a sin to lock them up does rejoice, but still, the place you live in is that much more drab and empty now that they’re gone".

My Recommended Place

It is the best movie theater in NZ - small and cozy, with sofas instead of theater seats, and freshly baked cookies at the intermission!
